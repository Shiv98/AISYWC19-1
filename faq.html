What is AISYWC?

The flagship event of IEEE India Council, IEEE AISYWC has always been a colloquium that brings together youth with professionals and experts with their ideologies tallying up to be the best exchange of wisdom and acuity. Founded in 2000, the AIS(YW)C has grown over the years to become the biggest event on the calendar for IEEE members in India. It is a conglomeration of inventors, professionals, entrepreneurs, visionaries and some of the greatest minds in the country. By bringing together amazing people from all walks of life, the AISYWC empowers its participants to be inspired, collaborate and innovate for tomorrow.

This year we will be conducting exciting events on latest technologies for students coming from different engineering backgrounds. This will help them connect to leaders and eminent speakers of their respective streams. We will also be catering to the needs of young professionals and senior members by having a platform for them to share their professional articles and views and help them get articulated to the crowd attending these symposiums. We also have some events in store for the women in engineering, a celebratory and an inspirational session for the women who've made it successfully and created benchmarks that we all look up to.

Where will AISYWC 2019 be held?

AISYWC 2019 is being hosted by CMR Institute of Technology in Hyderabad, India.

What is the Congress theme?

Acquire knowledge, Ideate to innovate, Strengthen skillset, Youngsters on a learning spree, Weaving networks to nurture, Collaborate to Change the world.

How do I get to CMR Group of Institutions?

Nearest Railway Station to the Congress venue are:
1. Hyderabad Railway Station, Old Lancer Lines, Secunderabad. It is approximately 24 km from the venue.

Nearest Airport to Congress venue:
1. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport. The approximated distance is 55.5 km from the Venue.

How can I register for the congress and what is the registration fee?

To register for the event, visit official website of AISYWC 2019- http://aisywc.tk/. Registration fees is 2500/- and includes Registration kit and food.

When does registration start and end?

The first phase of registration will start from 10 June 2019 and will be ending on 1 September 2019.

Will I be given some identification/access card to gain entry to Congress events?

Yes, an identification (ID) card will be provided to you at the registration desk on the first day of AISYWC 2019. You will be permitted to gain entry to any event during AISYWC 2019 only when wearing the identification card.
